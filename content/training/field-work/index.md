### Field Work in Dinosaur Provincial Park
Assisting in geological field work I helped gather stratigraphy data across the Dinosaur Provincial Park, assisting in PhD research.
- Utilized practical navigation skills in the maze like structures of the badlands coulees.
- Prepared the rock for stratigraphic section gathering using rck hammers, picks, and brushes to ensure proper exposure.
- Measured stratigraphic section using a jacobs staff with a high degree of precision within a couple cm of the GPS coordinates.
- Gathered high precision GPS points for the sections base and tops using real-time-kinematics and post-processing.

### Field Training Courses
Completed two comprehensive field readiness training class while at the University of Manitoba, taring for a wide verity of field scenarios across disciplines.
- Practiced practical fire starting for safety out in field situations.
- Exercised safety skills such as proper clothing and layering, bringing field essentials, and how to manage an emergency.
- Demonstrated when and how to shock-wrap and split in case of emergencies. 
- Displayed how to navigate using UTM maps and compass to triangulate positions and track distance, as well as other practical navigation techniques.
